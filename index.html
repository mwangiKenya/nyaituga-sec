About us

We remain committed to transforming the life of children, and preparing them to become resposible citizens.

Nyaituga Secondary school is a pubic day mixed secondary school, located in Mirangine subcounty, Nyandarua county. It was founded in 2013 to serve the community and deliver education facilities to the leaners from the communit.

We also prepare your child for life after high school

Our school prepares your child to higher learning level. This is achieved by ensuring that we provide enough guidence to your child before leaving our school after four years.

To achieve that, we also provide a platform that enables our learners to access usiful sites such as KNEC portal, KUCCPS portal, and HELB portal among others. Access them at the bottom of our website.
